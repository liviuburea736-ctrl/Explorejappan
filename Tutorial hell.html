The mighty warriors of old Japan

Shoguns of Japan

Shoguns are powerful military leaders who ruled Japan for over 700 years.

Samurai Warriors

Samurai were elite warriors known for their honor, discipline, and martial skills.

Bushidō

Bushidō is the way of the warrior, a code of conduct for samurai.

Culture in Japan

Tea Ceremony

The Japanese tea ceremony is a traditional ritual influenced by Zen Buddhism.

Kimono

Kimono are traditional Japanese garments worn on special occasions and ceremonies.

Most well know foods

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice, raw fish, and various toppings.

Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup dish that typically consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth.

Tempura

Tempura is a Japanese dish that typically consists of seafood or vegetables that have been battered and deep-fried.

Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu is a Japanese dish that typically consists of breaded and deep-fried pork cutlet, served with shredded cabbage and a tangy sauce.

Yakitori

Yakitori is a popular Japanese dish that consists of skewered and grilled chicken, often seasoned with a savory sauce or salt.

Sights

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is an iconic symbol of Japan and is the highest mountain in the country, standing at 3,776 meters (12,389 feet) tall. It is a popular destination for climbers and tourists alike, offering stunning views and a unique cultural experience.

Fushimi Inari Taisha

Fushimi Inari Taisha is a famous Shinto shrine located in Kyoto, Japan. It is known for its thousands of vibrant red torii gates that form a stunning pathway up the mountain behind the shrine.

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle is a historic castle located in Osaka, Japan. It is one of Japan's most famous landmarks and played a significant role in the country's history during the 16th century.

Nara Park

Nara Park is a large public park located in Nara, Japan. It is famous for its free-roaming deer that are considered sacred and are a symbol of the city. The park also features several historic temples and shrines.
